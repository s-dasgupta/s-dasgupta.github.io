--- layout: archive title: "Teaching" permalink: /teaching/ author_profile: true toc: true --- {% include base_path %} ## Teaching Philosophy I want students to think like engineers. I design learning that is active, ethical, inclusive, and grounded in real systems. I emphasize core concepts first, then connect them to real problems, data, and tools. I value formative feedback, growth over one-shot correctness, and continuous improvement informed by student and peer feedback. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0} ## Pedagogy and Methods - **Problem-Based Learning**, students work in teams on open-ended transportation problems that require modeling, analysis, and design decisions. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1} - **Flipped Classroom**, content is previewed before class, contact time is used for labs, discussion, and application. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2} - **Tool-centric labs**, Civil 3D, survey instruments, Python notebooks, traffic analysis packages. :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3} - **Simulation and XR**, I integrate simulation platforms and virtual or mixed reality when it improves system understanding and safety. :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4} - **Inclusive practice**, clear expectations, low-stakes checks for understanding, and targeted support for students from diverse backgrounds. :contentReference[oaicite:5]{index=5} ## Courses and Roles ### Undergraduate - **CE 260, Civil and Construction Surveying**, Lead Lab Instructor.
